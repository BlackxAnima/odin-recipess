Curry Chicken

Curry Chicken: A fragrant and comforting dish featuring tender chicken simmered in a blend of aromatic spices and creamy coconut milk. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of warmth and depth, making it a favorite for cozy dinners or casual gatherings.

This curry chicken recipe is a celebration of bold flavors and comforting aromas. To start, tender pieces of chicken are marinated in a blend of yogurt, turmeric, and garam masala, ensuring each bite is infused with a rich depth of spices. The marinated chicken is then gently simmered in a fragrant sauce made from onions, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, and a medley of spices including cumin, coriander, and chili powder. Coconut milk is added to create a creamy texture that perfectly balances the robust flavors of the curry.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs (about 680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated or minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (14 oz or 400ml) coconut milk
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the plain yogurt, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the bite-sized chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Heat vegetable oil or ghee in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  5. Add the finely chopped onion and saut√© until softened and translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle in the cumin powder, coriander powder, and chili powder, stirring well to combine with the onion-tomato mixture. Cook for another 1-2 minutes to toast the spices.
  7. Carefully add the marinated chicken pieces along with any remaining marinade to the skillet. Stir to coat the chicken in the spice mixture.
  8. Pour in the coconut milk, stirring well to combine everything together.
  9. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the skillet and let the curry simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and tender. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  10. Once the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency, remove the skillet from heat.
  11. Garnish the curry chicken with freshly chopped cilantro.
  12. Serve hot with steamed rice or naan bread, enjoying the aromatic flavors of this comforting dish.
