Garlic Steak

Garlic Steak: Juicy, flavorful cuts infused with robust garlic, seared to perfection for a mouthwatering experience.

This garlic steak recipe combines the bold flavors of garlic with succulent steak, creating a dish that's both simple and satisfying. To prepare, start by marinating your choice of steak with minced garlic , olive oil, salt, and pepper. Allow the steak to absorb the flavors for at least 30 minutes, ensuring each bite is infused with garlic goodness.
